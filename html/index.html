Moshe Fertel

By: Moshe Fertel, the Founder of NothingYet

Moshe Fertel is a 19 year old man who watches an inordinately large amount of Netflix. To his household and the TechnoOmniscient Big-Brother- so-called 'security for our own benefit' system, he would seem like an addict tittilated by the brilliance and creativity of the producers and actors whose TVshows he'd watch. But with honesty and a dash of shame, lately, that just wouldnt be so. It'd be true that he watched like an addict . He'd drink his WFPB (whole food plant based) iced coffee smoothie with all natural sweetener (whole dates) like an addict: in bed, propped up by a pillow, with netflix still playing. He'd do his best following the role of an addict when it came to the 'munchies', snacking on matza, as that was the only munchable snack in the house other than fruits, vegetables, and unsweetened nuts. And all the while, anyone labeling him an addict, would not be so. They would be levelheaded in the assumption, I'd agree to that. But so, no.
